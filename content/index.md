# Welcome to My Reflection Garden

This is a living, interconnected space for capturing and connecting ideas — not a blog where posts get buried, but a **network of thoughts** that grows over time.

## What is this?

Unlike a journal or blog, this Digital Garden:
- **Evolves**: Notes are never "finished" — they grow as you learn more
- **Connects**: Ideas link to each other using `[[Note Name]]` syntax
- **Reveals patterns**: Connections between notes spark "ah-ha" moments

## How to navigate

- Use the **graph view** (bottom left) to see how ideas connect visually
- Click any `[[link]]` in a note to jump to that concept
- Use the **search bar** (top right) to find anything
- Browse by **tags** or **folders** in the left sidebar
