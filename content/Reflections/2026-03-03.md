# Do more zagging than zigging

## Be Unique
Was listening to the compound affect today and the author emphasized that sometimes to get others attentions we need to zag while everyone is zigging. 
Go the extra mile, sent texts, meet people in person, find similiar hobbies, whatever it takes. Do it. People zag, zag and zag. If you zig people will notice. 
Yes 1 of 5 people might get annoyed but 4 of 5 will be impressed. 

## How do I apply this?
- For [[Future Employment]] I think flyng out to a job interview, coffee chats, setting up times to go visit an office, saying thank you, 
really understanding the basics of thier business, planning and talking to people way before interviews. 
- For [[Friendship]] its texting people and catching up, remembering the small things, calling people on drives, offering to babysit on a weekend
- For my [[Marriage]] its sending cute texts to Aubrey, buying flowers, planning fun dates, always being supportive, writing cute notes to her

## Questions
- How do you balance all these things at once?
- How can I zag over the course of my life with Aubrey?
- When should i just simply zig? Is there ever a time?
